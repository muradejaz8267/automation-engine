Physical examination reveals bilateral basal crackles and an S3 gallop. Echocardiography demonstrates concentric left ventricular hypertrophy with preserved ejection fraction. Cardiac catheterization shows elevated left ventricular end-diastolic pressure with normal end-systolic volume.

Explanation:

* (B) Preload is often elevated, not reduced

* (D) Contractility is relatively intact
