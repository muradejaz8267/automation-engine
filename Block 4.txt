Question 1
A 68-year-old man presents with sudden onset right-sided weakness and aphasia. CT head without contrast shows no hemorrhage. Last known well was 90 minutes ago. Blood pressure is 170/95 mmHg, glucose is normal, and he takes no anticoagulants.

Answer Choices:

(B) CT angiography first
