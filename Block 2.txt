Question 1
A 54-year-old man presents with 3 months of progressive fatigue, pruritus, and dark urine. He drinks alcohol socially and takes no medications. Examination shows scleral icterus and mild hepatomegaly. Labs reveal:
* ALP markedly elevated
* AST/ALT mildly elevated
* Total bilirubin 6.2 mg/dL (direct predominant)
* Anti-mitochondrial antibodies positive
Which additional finding is most likely present?
Answer Choices:
(A) Iron deposition in hepatocytes
(B) Interface hepatitis on biopsy
(C) Destruction of intrahepatic bile ducts

Explanation:
