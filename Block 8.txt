Physical exam reveals a single loud S2. Chest X‑ray shows a narrow mediastinum (“egg on a string”). ABG reveals severe hypoxemia with normal PaCO₂.
What is the most appropriate immediate management?
Answer Choices:
(A) Endotracheal intubation and mechanical ventilation
(B) Administer inhaled nitric oxide
